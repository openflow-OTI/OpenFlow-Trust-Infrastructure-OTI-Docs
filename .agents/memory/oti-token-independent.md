---
name: OTI token is independent from FLOW
description: OTI has its own standalone token/tokenomics, deliberately decoupled from OpenFlow's FLOW ecosystem token.
---

Ahmad initially planned a single ecosystem token (FLOW) shared across all OpenFlow Labs products, including OTI. After reviewing the tradeoffs, he decided OTI should have its own independent token instead.

**Why:** OTI has its own revenue model, its own customers (exchanges, DeFi protocols, marketplaces), and can operate with or without the rest of OpenFlow. Tying its fundraising/utility to FLOW's much larger, slower Attention Economy timeline (which requires a "Genesis Mode → governance-approved transition" before FLOW's token economy even activates) would throttle OTI's growth on a schedule that has nothing to do with OTI itself.

**How to apply:** Full tokenomics design (supply, allocation, vesting, sale structure, liquidity plan, revenue distribution) lives in `TOKENOMICS.md` in the docs. Do not conflate OTI's token with FLOW in any future planning — they are separate token economies belonging to separate (if related) projects. A future cross-ecosystem bridge between the two is possible but was explicitly deferred, not designed.
